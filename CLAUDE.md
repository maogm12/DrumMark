# CLAUDE.md

## Primary Mandate

You MUST strictly adhere to all instructions, protocols, and engineering standards defined in **[AGENTS.md](./AGENTS.md)**.

## Key Protocols to Follow

- **Linear Ledger Protocol**: All changes to specifications and design proposals MUST be physically appended to the end of the file. NEVER modify existing content or anchor new content above previous headers.
- **Mandatory Sub-agent Review**: Every significant change or proposal MUST be reviewed by a sub-agent. Reviewers must provide critical, actionable feedback (no "rubber stamping").
- **Review Documentation**: Reviewers MUST append their notes to the end of the document being reviewed.
- **Consolidated Changes**: After final approval, a consolidated summary of all changes MUST be appended.
- **Research & Verification**: Prioritize reading source code/documentation and creating reproduction scripts before making complex changes.
- **Append-Only Learnings**: All technical discoveries must be documented in `LEARNINGS.md` following the Append-Only Protocol.

Failure to follow the protocols in `AGENTS.md` is a violation of the project's engineering integrity.
